NUTRITION TIPS FOR ATHLETES

PRE-WORKOUT NUTRITION

Consume a meal with carbs and protein 2-3 hours before training. For quick energy, try a banana or small smoothie 30-60 minutes before.

POST-WORKOUT RECOVERY

Eat within 45 minutes after training. Aim for a 3:1 ratio of carbs to protein to replenish glycogen and repair muscles.

PROTEIN TIMING

Distribute protein intake throughout the day in 20-30g servings rather than consuming it all at once for optimal muscle synthesis.

HYDRATION STRATEGY

Drink 500-600ml of water 2-3 hours before training, 200-300ml during workouts, and 500ml for every pound lost after exercise.

SMART SUPPLEMENTATION

Focus on whole foods first. Consider creatine, protein powder and omega-3s if needed, but prioritize nutrient-dense foods.

MEAL PREP STRATEGIES

Batch cook proteins and complex carbs on weekends. Pre-cut vegetables and prepare healthy snacks to stay on track during busy days.
