FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What's the difference between the membership types?

+

Our Off-Peak membership offers access to the gym during less busy hours (before 12PM and after 8PM). Full Access gives you 24/7 gym access plus 8 group classes per month. Premium includes everything in Full Access plus pool access, unlimited group classes, and personal training sessions.

Can I freeze my membership?

+

Yes, members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year. For monthly memberships, a $15 freeze fee applies per month. Annual memberships can freeze for free up to 30 days, with a $15 fee for additional months.

Is there a joining fee?

+

Yes, there is a one-time joining fee of $49 for all new members. This fee covers your initial fitness assessment, membership card, and account setup. We occasionally run promotions that waive this fee.

How do I book classes?

+

Class bookings can be made through our mobile app, website, or at the front desk. Full Access members can book classes up to 7 days in advance, while Premium members can book 14 days ahead. We recommend booking early as popular classes fill up quickly.

Can I upgrade my membership?

+

Absolutely! You can upgrade your membership at any time. Just speak with our front desk staff or log into your account online. When upgrading, you'll only pay the prorated difference between your current plan and the new plan for the remainder of your billing cycle.
